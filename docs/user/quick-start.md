# Quick Start Guide

Welcome to RustChat! This guide will get you up and running quickly.

## Signing In

1. Navigate to your team's RustChat URL (e.g., `https://chat.yourcompany.com`)
2. Enter your email and password
3. Or use SSO if your organization has configured it

## Understanding the Interface

### Left Sidebar
- **Teams:** Switch between different teams
- **Channels:** Public and private channels you're a member of
- **Direct Messages:** 1:1 and group conversations

### Center Pane
- **Message History:** Scroll through past messages
- **Composer:** Type and send messages at the bottom

### Right Sidebar (RHS)
- Opens for threads, search results, and user profiles

## Your First Actions

### Set Your Status
Click your avatar to set your status:
- 🟢 Online
- 🟡 Away
- 🔴 Do Not Disturb
- ⚪ Offline

### Join a Channel
1. Click the **+** next to "Channels"
2. Browse public channels
3. Click "Join" on any channel

### Send Your First Message
1. Select a channel from the sidebar
2. Type in the message box at the bottom
3. Press **Enter** to send

### Format Your Messages
Use Markdown for formatting:
```
**bold text**
*italic text*
`code`
> quote
```
